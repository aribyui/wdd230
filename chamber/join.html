NP Membership

  • Access to resources and tools specific to the non-profit sector.
  • Discounts on chamber events and programs.
  • Ideal for non-profit organizations and charitable entities.
  • Inclusion in the directory of affiliated non-profit organizations.
  • Networking opportunities with similar organizations.
  • Personalized support and guidance for projects and campaigns.

Bronze Membership

  • Ability to promote the company on the chamber website and social media.
  • Access to exclusive networking events.
  • Discounts on services and training programs.
  • Newsletters and regular updates on business opportunities.
  • Participation in sectoral committees and working groups.
  • Perfect for small businesses and startups.

Silver Membership

  • Access to educational sessions and specialized workshops.
  • Additional benefits from Bronze membership.
  • Business consultancy and technical assistance.
  • Designed for growing medium-sized businesses.
  • Invitations to VIP events and exclusive dinners.
  • Opportunities for event sponsorship and chamber programs.

Gold Membership

  • All benefits of Silver membership.
  • Exclusive access to international events and trade delegations.
  • Priority seating at roundtable discussions and executive forums.
  • Strategic consultancy services and legal advice.
  • Special recognition at events and chamber publications.
  • The highest level of membership for outstanding companies.
