Design Principles Document

Lizandro Arizpe

Visual Herarchy

Playstation

playstation.com

I think this website complies with the Visual Hierarchy design principle. Upon entering for the first time a user to the page is attracted by the hero image found in the banner positioned on the right side, then, the visual inspection turns to the left to read the title and a brief description of the advertisement, below it there is a call to action button to click to learn more about the product. Furthermore, I think it also complies with other design principles, such as: Rule of Thirds, White Space and Clean Design, Proximity and Alignment.

Rule of Thirds

Sprite

sprite.com

In my opinion, this site complies with the Rule of Thirds principle, and in part also with other design principles such as: White Space and Clean Design, Proximity, Alignment and Contrast. I think it has a clean and orderly design, and use a Minimal principle.

Contrast

Cooler Master

coolermaster.com

I think Cooler Master is an innovative brand and that its website is also innovative, and it complies with the principle of Contrast. Other principles that I observed were: Visual Herarchy, has a hero image that tells the user what is the focal point, title, and call to action buttons. The design also seemed minimalist to me. I also, observer F patterns and Rule of Thirds principle.
